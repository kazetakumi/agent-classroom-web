Concept A
The Oracle
A 46px circle, fixed bottom-right of every screen — the only enclosed form on white. It breathes at 4.5s. Navigation returns to pure ← / → with no Sage mixed in. Tap the circle: it shrinks away as a full-screen Sage overlay rises from below. The arrival is a rising — something emerging from beneath the content. Dismiss: a pill handle at the very top. Pull or tap to send Sage back down, circle reappears.

EntrySlides up · translateY(100→0) · 420ms
DismissPill handle at top · tap or pull down

Concept B
The Interlocutor
The entire top header strip is Sage's territory. Warm background (#F9F9F7), warmer border (#E4E4DC). "SAGE" takes the name position. Tap the header: the Sage panel descends from directly beneath it, covering the screen content below. The header remains visible — above the threshold is still Sage. A "tap to close ↑" label appears. Tap the header again to retract. The entry moves in the same axis as the trigger: downward.
TriggerEntire top header · tap anywhere
EntryDescends below header · translateY(-110→0)
DismissTap SAGE header again · same gesture
HeaderAlways visible · shows close hint when open

Concept C
The Thread
A 44px left margin strip — always fixed. "sage" written vertically in Cormorant italic. All content shifts right to live beside it. Tap the strip: the Sage experience slides in from the left, covering the full screen. The spatial metaphor is consistent — you entered from the left, the margin returns you. On the Sage screen, the left strip becomes a ← back affordance in the same position. Tap it to slide everything back to the right.
TriggerFull-height left strip · "sage" vertical label
EntrySlides in from left · translateX(-100→0)
DismissLeft strip on Sage screen · ← arrow · tap
Spatial logicEntered from left · exits to left · consistent

The Ellipsis
Three middle-dots in Cormorant Garamond — spaced, not compressed into a Unicode ellipsis — breathing in slow sequence. One lights, settles, fades. Then the next. Then the third. Six seconds per cycle. Not a chat typing indicator — too slow, too calm for that. This is the weight of a teacher's pause before speaking. Sage is always mid-thought. Tap to hear it.

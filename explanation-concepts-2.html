Question · 3 of 12
A particle moves along the x‑axis with velocity v(t) = 3t2 − 6t. At what time is the particle at rest?
Selected
t = 0 only
Correct
t = 0 and t = 2
Explanation
The particle is at rest when velocity equals zero. Setting 3t2 − 6t = 0 and factoring gives t(3t − 6) = 0, so t = 0 or t = 2. Both are valid — the particle stops at the origin and again when it reverses.

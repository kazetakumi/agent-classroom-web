Concept 3
The Arrival
Nothing at first. The bottom of the screen is clean. Then — slowly, over about 60 seconds — Sage fades in. A good teacher doesn't lean over the moment you sit down. They wait. They read the room. Students who answer fast never see Sage at all. Students who sit with something long enough find that Sage has arrived, quietly, exactly when needed. The component is Sage's timing made visible.

Find the derivative of f(x) = 3x³ − 2x + 5.
A 9x² − 2  (your answer)
B 9x² − 2
C 9x² + 5
D 6x − 2
Explanation
1 Apply the power rule: d/dx(xⁿ) = n·xⁿ⁻¹ to each term separately.
2 For 3x³: bring down the 3, multiply by coefficient → 9x².
3 For −2x: derivative is simply −2. Constant 5 disappears.

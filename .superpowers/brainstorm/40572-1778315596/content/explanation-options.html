Find the derivative of f(x) = 3x³ − 2x + 5.
Your answer
You chose B: 3x² − 2. Correct: A: 9x² − 2
Why
Apply the power rule to each term: d/dx(xⁿ) = n·xⁿ⁻¹. For 3x³, bring the exponent down and multiply — 9x². For −2x, the derivative is simply −2. The constant 5 vanishes. Combined: 9x² − 2.
